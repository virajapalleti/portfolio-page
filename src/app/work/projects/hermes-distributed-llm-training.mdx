## Overview

Designed a distributed training system with GPU-level parallelism via custom CUDA kernels, cutting gradient-compression latency from 160ms to 3.5ms (46x) and communication bandwidth up to 13,000x versus standard distributed data parallel (DDP).

Introduces heterogeneity-aware sharding enabling LLM training across mixed hardware over commodity WiFi.
