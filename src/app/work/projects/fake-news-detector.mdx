## Overview

A supervised classifier that separates fake news from real reporting. Articles are cleaned, tokenised, and turned into TF-IDF feature vectors, then fed into a few scikit-learn models (logistic regression, naive Bayes, etc.) benchmarked against each other on accuracy and F1.

Built as a hands-on pass at applied NLP fundamentals before moving to heavier transformer-based approaches.

## Tech Stack

Python, scikit-learn, pandas, NLTK
